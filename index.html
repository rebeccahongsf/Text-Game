You wake up in a luxurious bed with a massive headache. You can't seem to recall where you are, how you got there, let alone who you are. Besides you lays a woman who appears to be unconscious. On the bedside table, there is a gun and wallet. A sleek jacket sits on the couch besides the main door, which is facing the east. As your eyes scan the room, there is a window to the north of you. In front of the window, there is a coffee table with what appears to be half eaten Chinese take out. There is also a door leading to the bathoom to the west. You feel the woman besides you begins to stir. You should probably get moving.

You have entered the bathroom. To the left there is a bathtub. On the right there is a sink and toilet. A tooth brush balances itself on the edge of the sink.

You step outside of the room. The hallway floor feels cold and there's a slight breeze coming from the right side of the hall.

The door locks behind you. I guess you won't be going back into that room.
